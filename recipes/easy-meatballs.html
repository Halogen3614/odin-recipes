Easy meatballs

Description

My husband makes these easy meatballs with simple ingredients like eggs, panko bread crumbs, garlic powder, and fresh basil; they come out flavorful and delicious every time! Kids love them, too!

If desired, add the cooked meatballs to your favorite sauce until submerged (this brings out the moistness).

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • Â½ cup panko bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine eggs, Italian seasoning, basil, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder in a large bowl. Add ground beef and panko bread crumbs; mix thoroughly.
  3. Shape beef mixture into about 40 meatballs (1 1/2-inch in size, a little smaller than golf balls) using a teaspoon. Place on ungreased, rimmed baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the centers, 10 to 15 minutes total, turning halfway through. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the centers should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
